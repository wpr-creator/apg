Build Your Argument

This station is about thinking and talking before writing. You'll build a complete essay argument step by step — position, evidence, reasoning, counterargument — then write just your thesis sentence.

How this station works

1You get a randomized essay prompt. Read it together out loud.
2Walk through 4 guided steps together — position, evidence, reasoning, and counterargument. Talk it through before typing anything.
3At the end, each person writes their thesis sentence independently. Swap and give one piece of feedback: does it take a real position with a reason?
4The goal is not to write the whole essay — it's to make all the decisions before the essay starts. That's the hardest part.
5If you finish early, hit "New Prompt" and run through a second argument.
