⚙️ The 3-Step Attack Method — Always Do These Before Reading the Question

1

What does this show?

Read/look at the source. Ignore the question. What is actually being shown or argued? Who are the actors? What's the direction of change or the relationship being depicted?

2

What's the argument?

Is this source critical of something? Celebrating something? Comparing two things? Showing a trend? What conclusion does the author/creator want you to draw?

3

What concept does this connect to?

NOW read the question. Which AP Gov concept does the stimulus illustrate, challenge, or extend? Most stimulus questions test whether you can bridge the source to the course vocabulary.
